How SMS Reminders Will Work

  1. Choose when reminders are sent (24h, 2h, custom)
  2. Personalize your message using placeholders like {{clientName}} and {{time}}
  3. Enable reminders for specific services or all bookings
  4. Automatically track replies and confirmations

Privacy Policy

How Solva currently handles data in this early preview stage.

Back to Home

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Solva is currently in an early preview state. This policy explains what information is and is not handled by the current experience.

Early Preview Status

Solva is still being developed. Features, flows, and storage behavior may change as the product evolves.

Local Data Storage

In the current preview, workspace data is intended to stay on your own device/browser (for example, via browser local storage) when used in demo contexts.

Uploaded Assets

If a business logo or similar setup asset is used in the current preview experience, it is stored locally in your browser/device context at this stage.

Accounts and Server Databases

At this stage, Solva does not provide user accounts on this landing experience and does not run a dedicated server-side customer database for this preview website.

Selling Personal Data

Solva does not sell personal data in the current MVP/preview stage.

Analytics and Cookies

This site currently does not run dedicated analytics tracking or advertising cookies.

Third-Party Links

Some links may direct to third-party services (such as feedback forms or preview hosting). Those services operate under their own policies.

Contact

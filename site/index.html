Got questions?

Gentle answers to the things you’re probably wondering.

Who is Solva really for?

Solva is for solo cleaners — especially women running their own business, balancing work and life, and needing a calm, simple system to manage it all.

Is Solva finished?

Not yet! What you see now (v1.1) is a focused, real working version — shaped by user feedback, and still evolving.

Can I use Solva with a team?

Solva is intentionally solo-first. Team support might come later, but right now it’s designed just for you — the owner, the doer, the one wearing all the hats.

How can I share feedback?

We’d love to hear from you. Use the feedback form to tell us what feels helpful, what’s missing, or what would make your day easier.
